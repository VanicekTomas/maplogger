MapLogger Builder

Turn your interactive map into a ready-to-run usability study. Everything happens in your browser.

1) Add your map files

Upload one ZIP that contains your map (HTML, JavaScript, CSS, images, data, fonts). We keep the folder structure and prepare every HTML page for logging. Anything that already loads from the web (e.g., map tiles) can stay as it is.

2) Write participant tasks

Add short tasks in the order you want people to do them (e.g., “Find the nearest pharmacy in Olomouc”). Participants will see them in a fixed bar at the top of the study, and they will be saved to the log.
